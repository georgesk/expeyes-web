Dielectric Constant

Dielectric constant of ordinary windows glass is measured. A capacitor is made by pasting aluminium foils on both sides to make a capacitor. The dimensions are 13 cm x 10.6 cm x 0.4 cm and the measured capacitance is 225pF. The dielectric constant calculated is 7.38. This experiments can be repeated using other insulating materials.
