Talking to the PC, via USB

On the microHOPE board, the Rx/Tx pins of ATmega32 are connected to the USB to Serial Converter IC. User programs also can use this path to communicate to the PC via the USB port. The PCRST jumper should be removed when communication programs are running, to prevent the reset signal to ATmega32. The following examples are provided

cro.c and cro.py

remote-adc.c and  remote-adc.py

pymicro.c  and blink.py
